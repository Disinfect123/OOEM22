Observable Occurrence Events Management & IT Services

Observable Occurrence offers Proximity Marketing solutions to ALL of its clients. Proximity Marketing allows localized wireless distribution of advertising content associated with a particular place. Transmissions can be received by individuals in that location which are non-intrusive and non-spamming.

Our Proximity Marketing solutions may be further targeted to specific groups within a given location, for example content in tourist hot spots may only be distributed to devices near the area.

Communications may be both time and place specific, e.g. content at a conference venue may depend on the event in progress.Uses of proximity marketing include (but not limited to!) distribution of media at concerts, information (weblinks on local facilities), gaming and social applications, and most importantly advertising
